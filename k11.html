Editors

  • Yudit
    • Source: ftp://sunsite.unc.edu/pub/Linux/apps/editors/X/yudit-1.3.tar.gz
    • Sort of a homepage: http://czyborra.com/yudit/
    • With such a .yuditrc in your homedirectory, yudit displays UTF-8 with unifont.
  • Emacs
    • With unicode.el by Otfried Cheong from http://www.cs.ust.hk/~otfried/Mule/, emacs displays UTF-8 with an intelligent mixture of fonts.
    • Warning: unicode.el buggy in connection with RCS.
